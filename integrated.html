Integrated Facility Management Services

Integrated Facility Management Services

We provide a comprehensive array of integrated services, covering essential soft services like housekeeping, sanitation, landscaping, and gardening, as well as critical hard services such as mechanical, electrical, and plumbing solutions. Our expertise extends to managing various waste types including solid, liquid and biomedical waste, alongside specialized services like pest control and effluent treatment. Additionally, we offer vital operational support such as production support, warehouse management, and airport management services including multi-level parking and traffic management.

Soft Services

Housekeeping and cleaning services: We provide a comprehensive range of commercial and industrial cleaning services across India, with a focus on quality, efficiency, and long-term service delivery methods.

Landscaping and gardening: We are responsible for the upkeep of landscapes and gardens at our customers' sites, including grass clipping, new garden installation, and general maintenance.

Disinfecting and sanitizing services: We thoroughly clean and disinfect all surfaces, equipment, and common areas. This often entails using specialized cleaning products, disinfectants, and equipment to remove dirt, grime, and bacteria.
